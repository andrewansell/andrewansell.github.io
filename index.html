Background

Welcome to my online site! I am a college-grad Program Manager at Microsoft on their Surface Devices. In December 2019, I graduated with a degree in Mechanical Engineering and another in Business Administration from the University of California, Berkeley.. This site is a portfolio of projects from my path learning about product development over the past 4.5 years.

I enjoy bridging the gap between engineering and user experience to create beautiful hardware products. Hardware excites me because of how tangible the impact is. Product release videos were actually what inspired me to study mechanical engineering. In college, I spent summers exploring different roles in product development: In 2017, I interned at Tesla as a mechanical engineer on the Model 3 during its launch. Most of my days were spent in CATIA and making design changes in response to manufacturing results. In 2018, I interned at Apple as a product design engineering program manager on Apple Watch. Most recently, last summer, I interned at Microsoft Surface as a development program manager on a next-gen accessory for Surface Go.

I'm originally from Glendale, CA, am an identical twin, and am half-Armenian and half-Kiwi (New-Zealander). I love travelling and learning about different cultures and my favorite food is Meditteranean :) I'm also interested in helping first-generation students succeed in the tech industry - and currently bring students with me along my journey through an educational youtube channel called The Tech Twins (youtube.com/thetechtwins).
